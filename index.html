Project Overview: The Privacy Challenge

Every click, every search, and every sign-up contributes to your **digital footprint**. This project dives into student perceptions and habits regarding data privacy. In an era of constant data breaches and pervasive tracking, being aware is the first line of defense. We aim to highlight how easy it is to compromise privacy and provide tools to regain control.
